Cariyāpiṭaka

Akittivagga

Dānapāramī 5

Mahāgovindacariya

“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
sattarājapurohito;
Pūjito naradevehi,
mahāgovindabrāhmaṇo.

Tadāhaṃ sattarajjesu,
Yaṃ me āsi upāyanaṃ;
Tena demi mahādānaṃ,
Akkhobbhaṃ sāgarūpamaṃ.

Na me dessaṃ dhanaṃ dhaññaṃ,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
Tasmā demi varaṃ dhanan”ti.

Mahāgovindacariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
