Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Daḷhavagga

6. Alīnacittajātaka

“Alīnacittaṃ nissāya,
pahaṭṭhā mahatī camū;
Kosalaṃ senāsantuṭṭhaṃ,
jīvaggāhaṃ agāhayi.

Evaṃ nissayasampanno,
bhikkhu āraddhavīriyo;
Bhāvayaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.

Alīnacittajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
