Dukanipāta

Upāhanavagga

8. Ekapadajātaka

“Iṅgha ekapadaṃ tāta,
anekatthapadassitaṃ;
Kiñci saṅgāhikaṃ brūsi,
yenatthe sādhayemase”.

“Dakkheyyekapadaṃ tāta,
anekatthapadassitaṃ;
Tañca sīlena saññuttaṃ,
khantiyā upapāditaṃ;
Alaṃ mitte sukhāpetuṃ,
amittānaṃ dukhāya cā”ti.

Ekapadajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
