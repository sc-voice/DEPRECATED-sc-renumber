Puggalapaññatti

Niddesa

10. Dasakapuggalapaññatti

Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā? Sattakkhattuparamassa, kolaṃkolassa, ekabījissa, sakadāgāmissa, yo ca diṭṭheva dhamme arahā— imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā. (1–5)

Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhā? Antarāparinibbāyissa, upahaccaparinibbāyissa, asaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, sasaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, uddhaṃsotassa akaniṭṭhagāmino— imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhāti. (6–10)

Ettāvatā puggalānaṃ puggalapaññattīti.

Dasakaniddeso.

Puggalapaññattipakaraṇaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
