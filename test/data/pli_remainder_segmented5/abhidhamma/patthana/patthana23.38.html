23.38. Ajjhattārammaṇattika, Kusalattika

Naajjhattārammaṇaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ajjhattārammaṇo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha …pe… avigate cha.
