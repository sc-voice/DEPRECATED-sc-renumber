15.46. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka, Kusalattika

Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.
