11.11. Vedanāttika, Vipākattika

Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ navipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
