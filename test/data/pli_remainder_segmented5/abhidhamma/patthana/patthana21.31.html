21.31. Hetuduka, Maggārammaṇattika

Nahetuṃ namaggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu maggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.
