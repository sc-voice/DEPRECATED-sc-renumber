Dukanipāta

Upāhanavagga

5. Vacchanakhajātaka

“Sukhā gharā vacchanakha,
sahiraññā sabhojanā;
Yattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
sayeyyātha anussuko”.

“Gharā nānīhamānassa,
Gharā nābhaṇato musā;
Gharā nādinnadaṇḍassa,
Paresaṃ anikubbato;
Evaṃ chiddaṃ durabhisambhavaṃ,
Ko gharaṃ paṭipajjatī”ti.

Vacchanakhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
