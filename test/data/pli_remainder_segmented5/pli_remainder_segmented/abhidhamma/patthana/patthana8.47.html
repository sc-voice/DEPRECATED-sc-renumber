8.47. Upādinnaduka

Naupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

Naupādinnañca naanupādinnañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
