10.16. Sanidassanattika, Upādānadukādi

Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ noupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …pe… nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
